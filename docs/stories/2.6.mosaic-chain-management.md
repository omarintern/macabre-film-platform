# Story 2.6: Mosaic Chain Management

## Status
Done

## Story
**As a** Creator, **I want** to create chains of my own posts with meta-titles, **so that** I can organize related works into cohesive series or collections.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. **Chain Creation**: When submitting a new post, creators can select "Start/Continue Mosaic" and choose a parent post from their existing works to create or continue a chain.
2. **Meta-title Management**: Creators can set a meta-title for the chain when creating the first child post, and this title appears on all posts in the chain.
3. **Chain Display**: Posts that are part of a mosaic chain display the meta-title prominently and show their position in the chain.
4. **Chain Navigation**: Users can navigate between posts in the same chain using previous/next links.
5. **Chain Modification**: Creators can modify the meta-title of their own chains, and changes are reflected across all posts in the chain.
6. **Firebase Integration**: All chain data is stored in Firebase Firestore with proper relationships and real-time updates.
